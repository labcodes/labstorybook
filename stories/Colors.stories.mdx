    <div className="columns">
      <div className="column">
        <p>The Mkt, or Marketing family brings together all color stacks for use in institutional digital or printed/physical pieces, for example: sketchbooks, social media, website, banners, furniture, etc.</p>

Both colors should preferably be applied in their default tones <code>-40</code>. If their use is not possible due to contrast issues, for example, use a darker shade like <code>-60</code>.</p>
        <p><span className="u-color-purple-60">Purple</span> is set to be used as a secondary color for mixing with <span className="u-color-teal-40">teal</span> and <span className="u-color-coral-40">coral</span>.</p>
        <p><span className="u-color-mustard-40">Yellow</span> is the additional color, for use mostly in the lighter shades (less or equal to <code>-40</code>), in pair with <span className="u-color-teal-40">teal</span>, <span className="u-color-coral-40">coral</span> or <span className="u-color-purple-60">purple</span>.</p>
