## Color
Tag's default color is mineral, but it can also be displayed in teal, purple, mineral, green, red, yellow, orange, pink, and cyan.

They also have two different skin tones, soft and intense.

To set a color use `lab-tag--[color]-[skin]`

The user may choose between two styles and tones as following.
