    <h2>Variations</h2>
    <div class="columns">
      <div class="column">
        <h3>tag</h3>
        <p>Used to indicate status, type, and other element variations. <strong>No actions are allowed for this type</strong> and focus is the only state styled.</p>
        <p>It may use the props <code>icon</code> or <code>thumb</code>.</p>
        <span>
          <Tag>Simple tag</Tag>
          <Tag icon="calendar">Tag with icon</Tag>
          <Tag thumbSrc="docs/tokens-spacing/10-spacing.png" thumbAlt="token image">Tag with thumb</Tag>
        </span>
      </div>
      <div class="column">
        <h3>dropdown</h3>
        <p>Used to switch status, type, or other element variations represented using a tag.</p>
        <p>It may use the props <code>icon</code>.</p>
        <span>
          <Tag dropdown>Label</Tag>
          <Tag dropdown icon="calendar">Label</Tag>
        </span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="columns">
      <div class="column">
        <h3>chip</h3>
        <p>Used to toggle actions on and off, and to represent single or multiple selection from a set.</p>
        <p>Props are not allowed and color skin is fixed to match selection state.</p>
        <div class="columns">
          <div class="column">
            <h6>Unselected chip</h6>
            <Tag chip>Unselected</Tag>
          </div>
          <div class="column">

            <Tag chipActiveMulti>Multiple selection</Tag>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="column">
        <h3>removable</h3>
        <p>Used as an entity of items picked from a list.</p>
        <p>It may use the props <code>icon</code> or <code>thumb</code></p>
