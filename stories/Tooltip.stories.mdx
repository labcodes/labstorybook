<h2>Colors</h2>

The tooltips color is defined in the `scss/variables/_settings.scss` file, and can be Black, Teal or Purple. Avoid using Purple tooltips if the project theme is Teal, and vice versa.

<img src="docs/tooltip/examples/colors.png" alt="Available colors for tooltips" />

<h2>Behavior</h2>

A tooltip is displayed upon hovering over it (desktop). It is not displayed on mobile.
Continuously display the tooltip as long as the user hovers over the element.

<div className="columns">
    <div className="column">
        <div className="columns">
            <div className="column">
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/edge do.png" alt="Good example of how to use a tooltip" />
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/edge dont.png" alt="Bad example of how to use a tooltip" />
            </div>
            <div className="column">
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/sidebar do.png" alt="Good example of how to use a tooltip" />
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/sidebar dont.png" alt="Bad example of how to use a tooltip" />
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div className="column">
        <ol>
         <li>It appears above and in the center of click targets* (default) and stay in place while cursor moves within the target </li>
         <ol type="a">
             <li>Tooltip disappears when cursor moves out of the target</li>
             <li>Tooltip remains while cursor moves within the target</li>
        </ol>
         <li>On sidebars, it should display either from the left or the right of click targets</li>
         <li>Over 150ms, tooltips fade in and scale up using the deceleration curve. They fade out over 75ms.</li>
        </ol>
        <br />
        <p className="li--red"><em>* When close to screen limits, keep the position of the tooltip visible with margins of 12px for all sides.</em></p>
    </div>
</div>

<h2>Long texts</h2>

Tooltip text should not be longer than 180 characters and 3 lines. A warn will be thrown in the console if the 180 chars limit is crossed.
