#### Behavior

A tooltip is displayed upon hovering over it (desktop). It is not displayed on mobile.
Continuously display the tooltip as long as the user hovers over the element. 

<div class="columns">
    <div class="column">
        <div class="columns">
            <div class="column">
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/edge do.png" />
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/edge dont.png" />
            </div>
            <div class="column">
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/sidebar do.png" />
                <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/sidebar dont.png" />
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="column">
        <ol>
         <li>It appears above and in the center of click targets* and stay in place while cursor moves within the target </li>
         <ol type="a">
             <li>Tooltip disappears when cursor moves out of the target</li>
             <li>Tooltip remains while cursor moves within the target</li>
        </ol>
         <li>On sidebars, it should display either from the left or the right of click targets</li>
         <li>Over 150ms, tooltips fade in and scale up using the deceleration curve. They fade out over 75ms.</li>
        </ol>
        <br />
        <p class="li--red"><em>* When close to screen limits, keep the position of the tooltip visible with margins of 12px for all sides.</em></p>
    </div>
</div>
