  <div className="columns">
    <div className="column">
      <p><strong>Do</strong></p>
      <ol>
        <li>Describes differences between similar elements</li>
        <li>Distinguishes actions with related iconography</li>
        <li>Provides advanced tips such as keyboard shortcuts</li>
        <li>Displays full information of ellipse texts</li>
      </ol>
    </div>
    <div className="column">
      <p><strong>Don't</strong></p>
      <ol>
        <li className="li--red">Don’t use tooltips to restate visible UI text</li>
        <li className="li--red">Don't use tooltips to display hidden actions</li>
        <li className="li--red">Don’t display rich text and imagery on tooltips</li>
      </ol>
    </div>

  <h2>Behavior</h2>
  <p>On desktop, a tooltip is displayed upon hovering over it. It is not displayed on mobile. Continuously display the tooltip as long as the user hovers over the element.</p>

  <div className="columns">
    <div className="column">
      <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/edge do.png" alt="Good example of how to use a tooltip" />
      <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/edge dont.png" alt="Bad example of how to use a tooltip" />
      <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/sidebar do.png" alt="Good example of how to use a tooltip" />
      <img src="docs/tooltip/examples/sidebar dont.png" alt="Bad example of how to use a tooltip" />
    </div>
    <div className="column">
      <ol>
        <li>By default the tooltip appears above and in the center of click targets* and stays in place while the cursor moves within the target</li>
        <ol type="a">
            <li>Tooltip disappears when the cursor moves out of the target</li>
            <li>Tooltip remains while the cursor moves within the target</li>
        </ol>
        <li>On sidebars, it should display either from the left or the right of click targets</li>
        <li>Over 150ms, tooltips fade in and scale up using the deceleration curve. They fade out over 75ms.</li>
      </ol>
      <p className="li--red"><em>* When close to screen limits, keep tooltip position visible with margins of 12px for all sides.</em></p>
    </div>
